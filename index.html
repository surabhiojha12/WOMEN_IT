What We Stand For

The enlightment of people is only possible by education.

We stand to provide free and accesible social education for women through information technology platform and the every growing internet platform and with this innovation we aim at complete education for women in both academic and vocational fields.

Our Vision

Our vision is a society of educated independent women

Our vision for women education is to empower women to work within a male dominated society and make most of thier opportunity and this can happen only through education both vocational and academic.
